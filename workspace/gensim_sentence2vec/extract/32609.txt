近年,視覚情報や聴覚情報に続き触覚情報の伝送を目的とする研究が盛んに行われている.これまで触覚伝送の分野では,システムの性能評価や安定性に関する研究が多く行われてきたが,実際に人がハプティックデバイスを用いた際にどのように感じ,またどの程度の再現性の劣化までを許容できるのかといった,人間の感覚に基づいた品質評価は着目されることはなかった.そこで,本研究では触覚伝送における主観品質評価を行い,さらに主観品質評価の実験で得られた結果を基に,触覚伝送における客観品質評価手法を提案する.
Recently, transmissions of tactile sensation have actively been researched following visual and audio in- formation. In conventional study, there is much research about performance evaluation of bilateral control systems. These methods just evaluate the performance of control systems, and can not evaluate the quality which human beings allow. Hence, this study researches a subjective quality assessment of the haptic communication. Because an operator evaluates the quality directly, the subjective quality assessment has a very important meaning for a quality index. However, the subjective quality assessment has some problems that the method needs many examinees and costs. For these problems, this study proposes a mapping method from the value of objective quality evaluation to the value of subjective quality assessment.
