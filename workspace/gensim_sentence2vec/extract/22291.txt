ミクサや逓倍器,発振器等の非線形回路の設計では基本波だけでなく高調波のレベルの設計も重要である.パラメータの抽出時と異なるバイアス条件では,出力される高調波のレベルの計算結果と測定結果が一致しなくなるという問題がある.本報告ではHEMTに適用可能な比較的簡単な,広範囲なバイアス条件での計算精度向上を図った大信号等価回路モデルを提案し,従来のモデルとのDC特性,入出力特性の比較を行う.
In the design of the nonlinear circuits as mixers, multipliers, oscillators and amplifiers, accurate estimation of the output level of the harmonic frequency is important as well as the output level of the fundamental frequency. We determine the model parameters at one measured bias and calculate the harmonics at the same bias condition, but we hardly calculate exact output level of the harmonic frequency at the other bias condition. We improve the simple large signal equivalent circuit model to able to calculate the output level of the harmonics accurately in a wide bias range for the HEMT. And we compare the DC curves and harmonic wave's output power characteristics calculated by using the improved model with these properties calculated by using the conventional models.
