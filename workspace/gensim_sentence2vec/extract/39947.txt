The title of lecture will be announced soon. .  ---------------------------------------------------------------------POSTER PRESENTATION  A poster session for GCOE RAs and PDs (as well as other students) is scheduled for Monday, January 16th, (first symposium day) in the afternoon. GCOE RAs and PDs are kindly requested to prepare a poster for this session. Authors are then requested to display their posters throughout the symposium, that is, from 12 PM of the first day until the end of the symposium.    The GCOE NGIT 2012 Best Student Poster Awards will be presented at the symposium.  Further details about the schedule of the poster session as well as instructions for poster creation and presentation will be announced by e-mail and at the symposium web site.  --------------------------------------------------------------------SELECTED STUDENT PAPER SESSION  The GCOE-NGIT 2012 Symposium will also hold a Selected Student Paper Session for students with international research activity. Several selected students will give 15 minute talks on their current research and interests during this special session.  --------------------------------------------------------------------GCOE Research Sessions and Interdisciplinary Collaboration Project SessionThe GCOE-NGIT 2012 Symposium will hold GCOE Research Sessions by the GCOE faculty members, where the members presents the summary of his/her GCOE research including the goal, activities, results, and future direction of their researches during the five years of research activities in GCOE-NGIT Program at IST, Hokkaido University. Every talk by a GCOE member, other than one by a GCOE ICP session speaker, is allocated 12 min talk slot with 3 min QA.We also have three GCOE Interdisciplinary Collaboration Project (ICP) Sessions selected from more than 15 GCOE ICPs concerning to research collaboration among four GCOE research subgroups, which are presented by the project leaders, Hidemi Watanabe for New Species Discovery project with subprojects on deep sea submarine HUBOS2K, Yoshikazu Miyanaga for High-Speed Information Search on FPGA, project and Masaharu Yoshioka for Knowledge Exploratory for Nanodevice Design and Manufacturing project. Each talk in GCOE ICP Session is allocated 25 min with 5 min QA time.
