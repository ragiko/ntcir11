連續音聲認識エキスパートシステムの構築にはセグメンテーションの方法,認識單位,ルールの仕組みなどが重要な問題である.本論文では,セグメンテーションの問題を避けるために不均一な單位を提案する.その認識單位は未定の數の音素を含んであり,兩端は定常部,その中間は遷移部である.不均一單位の構造は状態記述を用いてフレームとして表現され,認識ルールは自動的に生成される.認識の後には單位の重複性を利用して後處理を行っている.
In designing a speech recognition expert system,there are several aspects to be considered - segmentation method,recognition unit,structure of the rule,and the user aids.To avoid the difficulties in segmentation,a non-uniform unit is proposed.Every unit has its stationary point at each end of the unit,and transient part in the middle.The structure of the rules for phoneme recognition is designed according to the structure of the unit.Frame,a collection of semantic net nodes and slots,is used for representing speech recognition rules.The parameter trajectory is described by symbolic representation and fuzzy linguistic variables.We use redundancy of speech data to improve the performance of the recognition system in the post-processor.The system was tested with continuous Korean digit speech of unknown length,and recognition rate of 97% is obtained after the post- processing.
