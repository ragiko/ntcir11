音声認識実用化に向けて「頑健性」の必要性が叫ばれて久しい.頑健性を指向し,主に音響分析・音響モデルの側面から,いまなにが行われ,なにが課題とされているのかを考えてみたい.
Robustness has been thought as very important for a long time to apply speech recognition systems to the real world. Here let us consider what is the 'next step' to improve the robustness from the viewpoints of acoustic analysis and acoustic models.
