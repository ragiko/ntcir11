誤りを含む英文を2 つ選びなさい。
1 You are difficult to drive a car.
2 I wonder where he is now.
3 My friends and I are interested in the latest fashions.
4 Yumi has lost her pencil last week.
5 My sister received some books written in French. 
解答
1 形容詞 difficult は人間を主語にとれない。
4 明らかに過去を表わす単語(last week)と共に現在完了時制は使えない。
