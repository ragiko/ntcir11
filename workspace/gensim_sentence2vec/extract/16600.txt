平成20年度電気通信普及財団賞(テレコムシステム技術賞)受賞。
平成22年度日本ITU協会賞(国際活動奨励賞)受賞。
Jun Okamoto received B.S. and M.S. degrees in electrical engineering from the Science University of Tokyo in Japan in 1994 and 1996, respectively.
He joined NTT Laboratories in 1996 and has been engaged in the quality assessment of visual communication services.
Currently, he is working on subjective and objective assessment methods of audio and visual communication services. He has been contributing to VQEG (Video Quality Experts Group) since 2004.
He received the Telecommunications Advancement Foundation Award in 2009 and the International Telecommunication Union Encouragement Award in Japan in 2010.
