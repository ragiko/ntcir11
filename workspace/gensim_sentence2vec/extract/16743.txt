ミリ波帯OFDM伝送において,位相雑音による伝送特性の劣化を改善するため,判定指向形位相雑音補償(DD-PNC)を用いたOFDM受信機を提案する.ミリ波60GHz帯におけるWPANシステムの検討が盛んに行われているが,高速信号伝送を行うOFDM無線機をワンチップシリコンRF-CMOS ICで実現すると,位相同期ループ(PLL)シンセサイザにおける位相雑音レベルが高くなり,64QAMなどの多値QAM変調では伝送特性の著しい劣化が生じる.本報告では,復号器の出力結果を用いてDD-PNCと判定指向形チャネル推定(DDCE)を繰り返し行うOFDM受信機を提案する.受信機の初回処理では,OFDMシンボルに挿入されたパイロット信号を用いる従来の位相雑音補償を行う.繰り返し処理では,復号器の出力結果を用いてDD-PNCにより各サンプルにおける位相雑音を推定し,その推定値を用いて時間領域の受信信号に対して補償を行う.さらに,DDCEはその位相雑音が補償された受信信号を用いてチャネル・インパルス応答を推定する.DD-PNCとDDCEは,それぞれ異なったステップサイズのLMSアルゴリズムを用いて推定を行う.提案方式の性能を明らかにするため,計算機シミュレーションを行い,DD-PNCとDDCEを繰り返し行うことで,位相雑音補償と位相雑音により劣化したチャネル推定精度の改善を実現でき,符号化率3/4の64QAMにおいて,1MHzオフセットにおける位相雑音レベルが-80dBc/Hzにおいても良好な伝送特性を実現できることを示す.
In millimeter-wave OFDM transmission, this report proposes an OFDM receiver employing decision-directed phase noise compensation (DD-PNC) to improve the performance degradation due to the phase noise. Wireless personal area network (WPAN) systems in millimeter-wave 60GHz band are now being extensively studied, however, in the high bit-rate OFDM transceiver based on the one-chip Si RF-CMOS IC, the relatively large phase noise at the phase locked loop (PLL) synthesizer causes severe performance degradation in the higher level QAM modulation such as 64QAM. This report proposes the OFDM receiver which iterates DD-PNC and decision-directed channel estimation (DDCE) by exploiting the output of the channel decoder. In the initial process of the receiver, the conventional phase noise compensation is performed by using pilot signals inserted into each OFDM symbol. In the iterative process, the receiver estimates the phase noise at each sample by the output of the decoder and compensates for the time-domain received signal with the estimated phase noise. Moreover, DDCE estimates a channel impulse response by using the compensated received signal. DD-PNC and DDCE employ the LMS algorithm with different step-size parameters, respectively. Computer simulations are conducted so as to verify the effectiveness of the proposed receiver, and demonstrate that it can compensate the phase noise and improve the degradation of the channel estimation due to the phase noise by iterating both DD-PNC and DDCE, and that it can achieve an excellent transmission performance in the 64QAM modulation with the coding rate 3/4 under the condition that the phase noise level is -80dBc/Hz at the 1MHz offset.
