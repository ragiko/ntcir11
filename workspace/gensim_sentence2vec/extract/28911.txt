筆者らは先に, ローカル極座標を用いた直線検出手法および円検出手法を組合せ, 直線と円を効率的に検出する手法を提案した. この組合せ検出手法では, 直線を1次元, 円を2次元の投票配列で処理するので, 次元数の多い円検出処理の時間が支配的となり, 処理時間短縮を制約する要因となっていた. そこで今回の発表では, 円上部の形状特徴に基づくローカル極の選択によって, 円検出処理の投票配列を1次元化し, 両図形を高速に検出できる改良型組合せ検出手法を提案する.
We proposed the improved combination method to detect lines and circles by selecting local poles adequately on figures. In the combination method using the local polar coordinates, a 1-dimensional voting array is used for line detection and a 2-dimensional one is used for circle detection. The circle detection influences processing time strongly. Thus we considered to reduce array size in circle detection to 1-dimension, by selecting local poles based on upper shape of circles. Experiments were done on images consisting of lines and circles. As a result, we confirmed that both figures are detected fast by this improved combination method using a 1-dimensional array to vote.
