Univ. of Tokyo, Dept. of Mechano-Informatics, 7-3-1, Hongo, Bunkyo-ku, Tokyo Mirror neurons of primates' brain are regarded as embodiment of fundamental function for symbol emergence. We have proposed the mimesis system, which is one of implementation models of mirror neuron, based on Hidden Markov Models (HMMs).
