本稿では,変調信号のスペクトラムを周波数領域で直接分割して伝送する帯域分散伝送において,隣接サブスペクトラムの遷移域から各サブスペクトラム問の位相回転を推定し,タイミングずれに起因した位相傾斜によって生じる受信信号の位相歪みを特別なパイロット信号を用いずに補償するブラインド型位相補償方式を提案する.従来のパイロット信号による位相補償方式は,広帯域に信号を分散する場合に位相歪みが残留し伝送特性が劣化する課題があった.提案方式は帯域幅によらず所要C/N劣化量0.1dB以下となり,高精度に位相を補償出来ることを確認した.
This paper proposes a novel blind phase compensation method for Direct Spectrum Division Transmission. Direct Spectrum Division Transmission divides modulated signal into multiple sub-spectra in frequency domain and sends all sub-spectra from transmitter (Tx) side. In receiver (Rx) side, received signal undergoes phase distortion due to the timing difference between Tx and Rx side. The proposed method compensates this phase distortion by estimating phase rotation from transition bands of adjacent sub-spectra; no specific pilot signal is required. When sub-spectra are scattered across a wide frequency band, transmission characteristics of conventional method using pilot signals are degraded due to the residual phase distortion. Simulation results of proposed method show that degradation in required C/N is less than 0.1dB. This confirms that the proposed method realizes highly accurate phase compensation.
