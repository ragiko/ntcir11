本稿では,教師なしバッチ適応の性能向上を目指し,話者クラス音響モデルの検討を行った.複数の学習話者数が異なる話者クラスモデルを,認識時に尤度を基準として発話ごと選択し使用する.さらにこの認識結果を利用して教師なし適応の性能向上の検討を行った.以上の評価を日本語話し言葉コーパスを用いて行った.認識実験の結果,ベースラインの単語誤り率21.83%に対し,一般的なMLLR法による話者適応で18.88%,提案手法による話者適応で17.95%を得た.以上より,話し言葉音声認識において,提案手法が有効であることが分かった.
This paper describes a new method based on speaker-class models in order to improve the performance of an unsupervised batch-type speaker adaptation. In this method, an optimal speaker-class model is selected on a likelihood basis at recognition session. In order to improve the recognition performance further, an unsupervised speaker adaptation is carried out by using the recognition results of speaker-class model. The evaluation is conducted on CSJ (Corpus of Spontaneous Japanese). As a result, a word error rate of 21.83% was obtained in the baseline experiment. Meanwhile 18.88% was obtained by using conventional MLLR adaptation method, and 17.95% was obtained by using the proposed method. The results showed that the proposed method was effective for spontaneous speech recognition.
