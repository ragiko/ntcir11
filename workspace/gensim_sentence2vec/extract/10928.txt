高機能の自閉症スペクトラム障害児20名と健常児28名を対象に、音声から話者の意図を理解する能力を検討した。音韻情報(辞書的意味)が肯定的な短文と否定的な短文を、肯定的・否定的どちらかの感情を持って成人女性が話した音声を、皮肉や冗談を表す音声として用いた。韻律課題では話者の感情を、表情-韻律課題では話者の感情と話者の表情を判断した。その結果、両課題において自閉症スペクトラム障害児は課題の正答数が健常児よりも有意に少なかった。また、課題に文脈情報を加えても同じ傾向を示した。この結果は、音韻情報と韻律情報を分離する能力と、韻律情報と表情を適正に統合し話者の発話意図を理解する能力が加齢に応じ発達するものの、健常児よりも低いことが示された。
In order to investigate the communication problems in children with Autistic Spectrum Disorders(ASD), the mind-reading ability from speech was analyzed for 48 subjects (20 ASD group and 28 normal group) using spoken phrases with positive or negative phonetic (linguistic) meanings uttered by a woman with positive and negative prosody (emotions), and it used as a voice that showed irony and banter. Speaker's feelings were judged in the prosody task and speaker's feelings and speaker's facial expression were judged in facial expressions-prosody task. As a result, Though the correct score increases according to aging, ASD group's score was significantly less than normal group in both tasks. Moreover, the same tendency was shown though context information was added to the tasks. These results suggest that the ability of ASD group to understand speaker's hidden but true intensions by separating phonetic and prosodic information and integrating prosodic information and facial expression developed according to aging but lower than normal group.
