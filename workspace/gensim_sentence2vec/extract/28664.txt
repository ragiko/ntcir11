ABSTRACT スターリング機関の性能を予測するための新しいシミュレーション方式を開発した。この方式による解析結果と,試作したスターリング機関の出力特性を比較した結果,従来用いられてきた各種のシミュレーションモデルのいずれより精度の高い結果が得られることが確かめられた。また,この方式は,計算が比較的簡便であり,機関の設計,開発に非常に有用である。 A new method to estimate the performance of Stirling engines has been developed.The results analyzed by this method were compared with measured performance of the single cylinder piston-displacer type Stirling engine manufactured by way of experiment.It was made sure that the results by the new model were more accurate than these by the other models used for design of Stirling engines.Numerical calculation by this model is rather simple compared with the other models which have been generally used.The new model is very useful for design and development of Stirling engines.
