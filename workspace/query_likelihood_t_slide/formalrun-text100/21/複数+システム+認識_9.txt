This paper investigates a robot audition system focusing on: 1) spatially-high resolution, and
2) reduction of computational cost. For 1), we propose transfer function interpolation based on integration
of two linear interpolation methods in frequency- and time-domain. For 2), we propose optimal hierarchical
sound source localization to reduce the computational cost of peak searching in sound source localization.
We applied these techniques to simultaneous speech recognition for a robot and successfully conrmed their
validity.
